Privacy Policy for FitTimer

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

Thank you for using FitTimer ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information Collection and Use

FitTimer is designed to respect your privacy. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we actively collect identifiable personal information such as your name, email address, or physical location.

Local Data Storage

Your workout history, language preferences, and timer presets are saved strictly locally on your device. We do not transmit, sync, or store this data on any external servers. You have complete control over this data and can delete your history directly within the App at any time.

2. Third-Party Services (AdMob)

The "Free" version of the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements to keep the app free. AdMob may collect and use information (such as your device's Advertising ID and IP address) to provide personalized ads and analyze ad performance.

  • Google AdMob: AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google LLC. For more information on how Google uses the collected information, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms page.

Note: If you use the "Pro" version of the App, no advertisements are displayed and no advertising-related tracking occurs.

3. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages, but does not specifically target anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the developer email provided on the Google Play Store app listing.
